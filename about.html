About Plus Hospital

PlusCare Hospital is a leading healthcare facility dedicated to delivering modern, affordable, and accessible medical care for all. Our mission is to blend compassionate service with cutting-edge technology to ensure every patient receives the best care possible.

Established in 2015, PlusCare Hospital has grown to include specialists in over 10 departments, offering quality health solutions across communities in Ghana and beyond.

Our Services Include:

  • ✔ Advanced digital health records system
  • ✔ 24/7 emergency and laboratory services
  • ✔ Highly qualified doctors and nurses
  • ✔ Patient-first approach to healthcare
